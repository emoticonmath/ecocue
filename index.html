Who Are We?

We are a group of programmers based out of Dublin, CA: Muhammad Tariq, Thomas Meagher, and Mahir Yasar, who are eager to improve the lives of the populace, as well as impact the world in a positive way. As of 2018, we are all taking Computer Science Principles where we learn the foundations of Computer integrations and engineering. We built this product for a project in which we were to solve a problem that existed in the world. After some brainstorming, we decided to build a eco-friendly grill because grilling is a major factor for air pollution. In the future, we hope to greatly expand on this idea in order to improve on the design and reliability.
